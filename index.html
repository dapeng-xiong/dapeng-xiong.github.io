Dapeng is an Associate Professor in the School of Biological Science & Medical Engineering at Southeast University. He earned his Ph.D. from Tsinghua University and subsequently conducted postdoctoral research at Cornell University. With a diverse academic background in Information and Computational Science, Computer Science and Technology, and Biology, his research focuses primarily on AI-driven computational biology. He has published numerous papers as first author or corresponding author in top-tier journals such as Nature Biotechnology, Pattern Recognition, and Communications Biology.
